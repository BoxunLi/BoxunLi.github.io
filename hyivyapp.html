HYIVY APP DESIGN

May - August 2020

INTRO

This app was intented to be used with the medical device Hyivy is planning to release. During therapy sessions, the app will receive data from the device and represent it in a user-friendly way. The app can also be used to track process, review previous session records as well as serving educational purposes to those new to the thearapy.

PROBLEM

A problem that was identified to me was that a lot of patients going through the post surgical treatments give up and abandon the treatment process because they feel like it is not working. To tackle this problem, the app had to be designed to give off an extremely positive energy as well as somehow deliver encouragement and some type of incentives for carrying on with the therapy.

PROBLEM

To make the app deliver a bright and positive vibe, I included many round edges, soft gradients, light pastel colours, as well as personal greetings from the app to create a friendly atmosphere.

The app was also designed keeping in mind that many of the users are of older women. So many functions inside the app is colour coded, and navigation is intuitive and easy to prevent creating confusion and to adapt to the target audience.

The creative data visualization and representation also makes the app visually appleasing and easier to understand by the targetted users.

INCENTIVE

As the incentive to excourage the users to follow through with the therapies, I designed a simple and cute game that gives rewards both in game and coupons in real life to give patients extra drive to continuing their treatment. The game’s aesthetics is very simplistic and cute, which appeals to the target group of users.
