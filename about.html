BOXUN LI

Greetings!

I am a second year Mechatronics student at the University of Waterloo and an aspiring designer specializing in web-design, branding, mobile apps, interactive user-interface, illustrations, fashion and product prototype.

On top of designing I also can be a consultant and help provide creative visions and directions for projects as well as provide advice and critique.

Towards the team, I aim to be as direct and straightforward as possible. My approach to designing is fast paced and practical. I’ll move thoughtfully, and efficiently in the most appropriate direction, and rework it until we both agree it looks and feels right, and will perform as intended.
