MERAKI DISTRICT CLOTHING

November 2016 - Mar 2019

meraki [may-rah-kee]

(n.) the soul, creativity, or love put into something; the essence of yourself that is put into your work

Meraki district is a clothing brand my brother and I started in 2016. It’s a brand through which we convey our artistic opinions and passions through pieces of clothing.

We worked through the designs digitally, after which we handmake each of our products.

We ran the brand for 2 and a half years, creating over 25 different designs and making over 400 sales through both online and pop up booths in events.
