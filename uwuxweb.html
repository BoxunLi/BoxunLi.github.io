Instead of using the old intervention of medical software set-up, which is very rigid and unintuitive. I chose a very simplified and minimalistic approach and created a simplistic sign-up and dashboard interface.

Through the simplistic layout, medical practitioners can get a lot done with very little clicks as everything they need is either right in front of them or very few clicks away.

With the pandemic going on around us, this project can greatly benefit patients and doctors to deliver accurate diagnoses even when they are far away.
